# Bo mau Obsidian cho BA / PO / Test / Nhan vien trien khai

Bo mau nay duoc thiet ke de dung nhanh trong `Obsidian` cho 4 nhom vai tro:
- BA
- PO
- Test
- Nhan vien trien khai

## Muc tieu

- Ghi nhan thong tin nhat quan
- De review, de handover, de truy vet
- Tach bach muc tieu, pham vi, quy tac, rui ro va viec can xu ly

## Cach dung nhanh

1. Bat `Core plugin` co ten `Templates` trong `Obsidian`.
2. Cau hinh `Template folder location` tro den thu muc `Templates`.
3. Tao note moi va chen mau phu hop.
4. Doi cac placeholder nhu `[Can dien]` thanh noi dung thuc te.

## Nguyen tac dung bo mau

- Moi note chi nen co 1 muc tieu chinh.
- Phan nao chua chac thi ghi ro la `Gia dinh` hoac `Open question`.
- Khong tron `yeu cau`, `giai phap`, `quyet dinh tam` trong cung 1 muc neu chua thong nhat.
- Neu noi dung lien quan du lieu nhay cam, chi ghi muc can thiet va an thong tin nhan dang khi can.

## Bo mau chuyen sau

- Neu lam du an y te va ket noi thiet bi, dung them file `00-README-Bo-mau-Y-te-Ket-noi-thiet-bi.md`.
- Cac mau trong `Templates/Y-te-Ket-noi-thiet-bi` uu tien cho BA trien khai, Test va doi ho tro tai hien truong.
